Can You help?

Prototype of an algorithm based on a simple, testable principle:

1 > 0 – the distinction between content that supports life, learning, mental health (high frequency) and content that leads to trauma, addiction, and social decay (low frequency).

For minors, the default setting is 1 – only high‑frequency material is accessible. For adults, thresholds can be set by the account holder.

It uses measurable indicators of frequency (coherence, entropy, emotional valence) and can be trained on open data...
